# Bipolar and Related Disorders
A group of disorders in which episodes of [[mania]] alternate with periods of [[depression]].
___
# Depressive Disorders
A group of disorders characterized by feelings of **extreme sadness** and **worthlessness**, along with **reduced interest in previously enjoyable activities**.
- **Major Depressive Disorder**
- **Premenstrual Dysphoric Disorder** (PMDD), a more severe form of **Premenstrual Syndrome** (PMS), which is **not** a psychiatric disorder.
___
# Schizophrenia Spectrum and Other Psychotic Disorders
**Psychotic** disorders that cause **detachment form reality**. People with these diagnoses experience **delusios**, **hallucinations**, and **disorganized thinkinng and speech**.
- **Schizophrenia**
___
# Anxiety Disorders
Anxiety involves **focusing on bad or dangerous things** that could happen and **worrying fearfully and excessively** about them.
- **Generalized Anxiety Disorder** (GAD)
- **Panic Disorder**
- **Phobias**
___
# Obsessive-Compulsive and Related Disorders
People with these disorders experience **repeated and unwanted urges**, **thoughts,** or **images** (obsessions) and feel driven to **taking repeated actions in response** to them (compulsions).
- **Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder** (OCD)
- **Hoarding Disorder**
- **Hair-Pulling Disorder** (Trichotillomania)
___
# Trauma and Stressor-Related Disorders
Psychiatric disorders that develop **after stressful or traumatic life events**.
- **Post-traumatic Stress Disorder** (PTSD)
- **Acute Stress Disorder**
___
# Dissociative Disorders
Disorders in which a person's **sense of self is disrupted**.
- **Dissociative Identity Disorder**
- **Dissociative Amnesia**
___
# Somatic Symptoms and Related Disorders
A person with **distressing and incapacitating physical symptoms with no clear medical cause**.
- **Somatic Symptom Disorder** (prev. *hypochondriasis*)
- **Factitious Disorder**
___
# Personality Disorders
Lasting **patterns of emotional instability and unhealthy behaviors that seriously disrupt daily living and relationships**.
- **Borderline Personality Disorder** (BPD)
- **Antisocial Personality Disorder**
- **Narcissistic Personality Disorder**
___
# Eating and Feeding Disorders
Disorders characterized by alterations in eating patterns and disturbances in body image interfering with relationships and occupational functioning.
- **Anorexia Nervosa** (AN)
- **Bulimia Nervosa** (BN)
- **Binge Eating Disorder** (BED)
- **Other Specified Feeding and Eating Disorder** (OSFED)
- **Pica**
- **Rumination Disorder**
- **Avoidant/Restrictive Food Intake Disorder** (ARFID)
- **Unspecified Feeding or Eating Disorder** (UFED)
- Others:
	- **Muscle Dysmorphia**
	- **Orthorexia Nervosa** (ON)
___
# Elimination Disorders
Inappropriate elimination of urine or stool either voluntarily or involuntarily.
- **Enuersis** (Bed-wetting)
___
# Sleep-Wake Disorders
Sleep disorders that affect the ability and quality of sleep.
- **Insomnia Disorder**
- **Nightmare Disorder**
- **Sleep Apnea**
- **Restless Legs Syndrome**
___
# Sexual Dysfunctions
Disorders of sexual response
- **Premature Ejaculation**
- **Erectile Disorder**
- **Female Orgasmic Disorder**
___
# Paraphilic Disorders
"Sexual-interest" disorders.
- **Sexual Sadism Disorder**
- **Voyeuristic Disorder**
- **Pedophilic Disorder**
___
# Gender Dysphoria
Disorders that stem from the distress that goes with **a person's stated desire to be a different gender**. The diagnostic criteria in this group differ between age groups.
___
# Neurocognitive Disorders
Affected **thinking** and **reasoning**. These disorders involve **delirium**.
- **Dementia of the Alzheimer Type** (DAT)
- **Traumatic Brain Injury** (TBI)
___
# Neurodevelopmental Disorders
Disorders that often begin in infancy or childhood.
- **Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder** (ADHD)
- **Autism Spectrum Disorder**
- **Learning Disorders**
___
# Substance-Related and Addictive Disorders
Problems with the **excessive use of alcohol**, **opioids**, **recreational drugs**, **hallucinogens**, and other (6) types of drugs. This also includes addictions to **gambling**, **gaming**, and **sex**.
___
# Disruptive, Impulse-Control, and Conduct Disorders
Difficulty in **emotional and behavioral self-control**
- **Kleptomania**
- **Intermittent Explosive Disorder**
___
# Other Mental Disorders
Disorders that don't classify as any of the previously listed disorders.
___
